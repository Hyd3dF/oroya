Rules & Safety

Community Guidelines

These guidelines describe what is and is not acceptable when using the OROYA application and its associated support channels.

Updated: March 19, 2026 Community document

Respectful Use

Use OROYA in a lawful, respectful, and responsible way. Abuse of the service, support channels, or reporting systems is not permitted.

Do not harass, threaten, intimidate, or target other people through submitted content, profile information, or support messages.

Prohibited Content

You must not upload photos, text, or other material that falls into any of the following categories:

  • Illegal, exploitative, or violent content
  • Harassment, hate speech, threats, or bullying
  • Sexually exploitative or abusive material
  • Impersonation, fraud, scams, or misleading submissions
  • Copyrighted or private material used without authorization

Platform Misuse

Do not attempt to damage, overload, scrape, reverse-engineer, or interfere with OROYA, its backend systems, AI integrations, or supporting cloud services.

Automated or deceptive behavior intended to manipulate the service or generate abusive traffic is not allowed.

Enforcement

We may review reported or detected violations and take action including warnings, content removal, feature restrictions, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination.

Where necessary, we may preserve records and cooperate with legal obligations, safety reviews, or abuse investigations.

Report abuse or misuse

If you need to report prohibited content, harmful behavior, or misuse of the service, please use the contact page.
