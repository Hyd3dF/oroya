Support

Contact

Reach out to the OROYA team for support, legal questions, privacy inquiries, data deletion requests, or App Review communications.

Updated: March 19, 2026 Support document

Contact Details

  • Email: support@oroya.xyz
  • Website: https://oroya.xyz

What to Include

To help us respond efficiently, please include the following in your message:

  • Your account email address or identifier
  • A short description of the issue or request
  • Any relevant details about uploaded photos, stored results, or AI-generated content
  • For privacy or deletion requests, clearly state the action you want us to take

Response Times

We aim to review and respond to support and legal requests within a reasonable period. Some requests may take longer where identity verification, data review, or legal assessment is required.

If additional information is needed, we may contact you before completing the request.
