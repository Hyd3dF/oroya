Privacy Policy

This policy explains how OROYA collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you upload photos, receive AI-generated results, and use the mobile application.

Updated: March 19, 2026 Privacy document

Information We Collect

When you use OROYA, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — Registration details such as email address, username, or password, whether provided now or through future account features.
  • Photos — Images you upload through the mobile application for AI analysis and content generation.
  • AI-generated content — Descriptions, titles, hashtags, text outputs, and other results generated by the AI based on your uploaded photos.
  • Technical data — Device type, operating system, crash logs, and usage diagnostics collected automatically.
  • Communications — Messages or requests you send through our support channels.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate the service and process your uploaded photos through AI analysis.
  • To generate and deliver descriptions, titles, hashtags, and other text-based results.
  • To store your photos and AI-generated content so you can access them within the application.
  • To improve the quality, reliability, and security of the service.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate with you about your account.
  • To prevent misuse, fraud, and violations of our terms.

When you upload a photo for analysis, that photo may be processed by our internal systems and sent to a third-party AI service provider to generate the requested output.

Sharing with Third Parties

We may share your data with third-party service providers when necessary to deliver the core functionality of the application:

  • AI providers — Your uploaded photos may be sent to third-party AI services for analysis and content generation. The specific provider may change over time as we improve the service.
  • Cloud infrastructure — We use cloud providers to store uploaded photos, generated content, result records, and account data.
  • Service providers — We may use additional providers for analytics, security, and operational purposes.

We do not sell your personal information. Third-party providers are used strictly to operate, secure, and improve the service.

Data Storage and Retention

Your uploaded photos are stored in our database and connected storage systems. AI-generated descriptions, titles, hashtags, and result records are also stored so you can view your past outputs within the application.

Account information, including login credentials provided now or through future account features, is stored securely on our servers.

We retain data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain account history, comply with legal obligations, and support legitimate operational needs. When data is no longer needed, we take steps to delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable law and our legitimate operational obligations.

  • Access — You may request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — You may request deletion of your account data, uploaded photos, and stored AI-generated results through our contact page.
  • Support — You may contact us at any time with privacy questions or concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the application after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact us about privacy

For privacy questions, access requests, or deletion requests, please use the contact page.
