Application Overview

Application Information

A clear overview of how the OROYA mobile application works, what data it processes, how that data is stored, and which third-party services may be involved.

Updated: March 19, 2026 Product information

What Is OROYA

OROYA is a mobile application that helps users generate text-based content from their photos. Users upload a photo, and the application uses artificial intelligence to analyze the image and produce outputs such as descriptions, titles, hashtags, and captions.

The generated content is returned to the user within the application and may be stored for later access.

How It Works

The application follows a straightforward process:

  1. The user selects and uploads a photo through the mobile application.
  2. The photo is sent to an AI system for analysis and content generation.
  3. The AI analyzes the photo and produces text-based outputs such as descriptions, titles, and hashtags.
  4. The generated results are returned to the application and displayed to the user.
  5. Both the uploaded photo and the generated content may be stored in the database for future access.

Data Collected and Processed

The following types of data may be collected and processed by OROYA:

Photos User-uploaded images submitted for AI analysis
AI Output Generated descriptions, titles, hashtags, and text content
Account Data Email, login credentials, and profile information (current or future)
Technical Data Device type, OS, crash logs, and usage diagnostics

Third-Party Services

OROYA relies on third-party services to provide its core functionality:

  • AI analysis providers — User-uploaded photos may be sent to third-party AI services for image analysis and content generation. The specific AI model or provider may change over time as the service evolves.
  • Cloud infrastructure — We use cloud providers to store uploaded photos, generated content, and associated data securely.
  • Operational services — Additional providers may be used for analytics, monitoring, and security purposes.

Third-party providers are used strictly to operate and improve the service. We do not sell user data to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored on secure servers. This includes:

  • Uploaded photos and associated metadata
  • AI-generated text content (descriptions, titles, hashtags)
  • Account information and login credentials
  • Result records linking photos to their generated outputs

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect stored data. Account credentials, whether provided through current or future registration features, are stored securely on the server.

Purpose of Data Processing

Data is collected and processed for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery — Processing photos and generating AI-based content
  • Content storage — Allowing users to access past results within the app
  • Quality improvement — Monitoring and improving service reliability
  • Security — Protecting the platform and preventing misuse
  • Support — Responding to user inquiries and managing accounts

User Rights

Users have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data. Deletion requests can be submitted through the contact page and may cover uploaded photos, stored AI-generated content, and account information.

For full details on user rights and data handling practices, see our Privacy Policy and Data Deletion Policy.

Questions or concerns

For additional information about the application, data practices, or compliance inquiries, please contact us directly.
