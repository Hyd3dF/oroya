Data Deletion

How to request deletion of your account data, uploaded photos, AI-generated descriptions, and stored result records.

Updated: March 19, 2026 Deletion document

How to Request Deletion

You may request deletion of your data by contacting OROYA through the contact page on this website. Please include the following information:

  • The email address or identifier linked to your account
  • A statement that you are requesting deletion of your data
  • Any additional information needed to verify your identity

What Deletion Covers

Subject to applicable law and technical constraints, deletion may include:

  • Account information and login credentials
  • Uploaded photos stored in our systems
  • AI-generated descriptions, titles, hashtags, and result records
  • Support history and communication records

Some data may temporarily remain in backups, logs, or security systems for a limited period after the deletion request is processed.

When Data May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, security, abuse investigations, or enforcement of our terms and policies.

Where retention is necessary, we retain only the minimum information required for those purposes.

Submit a deletion request

Use the contact page and include the account identifier associated with your uploaded photos or stored results.
