About OROYA

OROYA is a mobile application that helps users generate text-based content from their photos using artificial intelligence. Users upload a photo, and the app produces descriptions, titles, hashtags, and captions powered by AI analysis.

This website serves as the official policy and support center for the OROYA application. All legal documents, privacy disclosures, and support information are published here.
